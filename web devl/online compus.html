About Us

At the Kenya Youth Centre for Online Business, we all come to work everyday because we want to solve the biggest problem facing the youth, that is, unemployment. Youths don't know that they can build their freelance business and attain financial freedom from the comfort of their home. Lot's of businesses have gone virtual creating uncountable opportunities which you can grab as long as you have the skills.
Our mission is to enlighten the youth on available opportunities and how they can be part of this growing virtual space.


~ Our Vision ~

Empower the youth by training them on the various virtual job opportunities


Our Services

Online Courses

We offer full virtual training on a course of your choice. Our tutors carry out a vigorous training routine to ensure succesful acquisition of skills for the mentees. You will also get a certificate after successful completion of the course.

Training on Bidding

After picking a course, we will also train you on how to bid for jobs on online freelance platforms. Here you will get to learn the do's and don't's of successful bidding. We will also provide proposal samples for you to use for practice purposes.

Work From Home

We've partnered with various organizations to ensure that our trainees/mentees get jobs and start earning immediately. After succesful completion of the training, we will help you land jobs on various platroms and work from the comfort of your home.
